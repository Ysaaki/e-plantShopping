Paradise Nursery

Paradise Nursery is a simple React e-commerce application designed for browsing and purchasing indoor plants. Plants are organized into helpful categories like Air Purifying, Medicinal, and Low Maintenance.

What It Does

Browse Plants:         View plant details, images, descriptions, and prices.
Add to Cart:           Add your favorite plants to the shopping cart with a single click.
Live Counter:          See the total number of items in your cart update instantly in the navigation bar.
Manage Quantities:     Open the cart to easily increase, decrease, or completely delete items.
Dynamic Calculations:  Automatically calculates individual item costs and the grand total price for checkout.
